Course Structure

  1. Operating Systems (Windows, MacOS, Linux)
  2. File Management (creating, copying, moving, deleting files and folders)
  3. Basic Text Editing (Microsoft Word, Google Docs, etc.)
  4. Email (setting up an email account, composing and sending emails)
  5. Web Browsing (Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, etc.)
  6. Internet basics (searching the web, online security, privacy)
  7. Basic Office Suite (Microsoft Office(MS Excel , PowerPoint ), Google Suite, etc.)
  8. Online Storage (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)
  9. Basic hardware components and their functions
  10. Installing and uninstalling software
  11. Basic Networking (Wi-Fi setup, sharing files and printers)
  12. Cybersecurity and online safety
  13. Backup and Recovery
  14. Keyboard Shortcuts
  15. Virtual Meetings (Zoom, Skype, etc.)
